HYUNDAI CRETA — MAINTENANCE GUIDE (2015-2023 MODELS)

OIL CHANGE INTERVAL
- 1.5L Petrol (2020+): Every 10,000 km or 12 months
- 1.5L Diesel (2020+): Every 7,500 km or 12 months
- 1.4L Turbo Petrol (2020+): Every 10,000 km
- Older 1.4L Petrol / 1.6L variants: Every 5,000-7,500 km
- Capstone estimation: 6,000 km average interval

SERVICE SCHEDULE
- 1st free service: 1,000 km or 1 month
- Regular service: Every 10,000 km (new) or 7,500 km (older)
- Major service (30,000 km): Spark plugs, air filter, brake fluid check
- Major service (60,000 km): Coolant flush, fuel filter
- Major service (80,000 km): Timing belt check (diesel variants)

TIMING BELT/CHAIN
- 2020+ Creta 1.5L petrol: Timing chain — no replacement needed
- 2020+ Creta 1.5L diesel: Timing belt — inspect at 80,000 km, replace by 100,000 km
- 2015-2019 Creta 1.6L diesel: Timing belt — replace at 80,000 km

TYRE LIFE
- 40,000-50,000 km standard
- Benchmark: 40,000 km per set

BRAKE PAD LIFE
- Front disc pads: 30,000-40,000 km
- Benchmark: 30,000 km per set

KNOWN ISSUES BY YEAR
- 2015-2018 Creta: Vibration at highway speeds due to wheel balancing issues
- 2018-2020 Creta: CVT variants reported jerky acceleration at 50,000+ km
- 2020+ Creta (BS6): Generally well-reviewed; some DCT (7-speed) transmission reports of hesitation
- 1.5L Diesel (2020+): Particulate filter cleaning recommended at 60,000 km in city use
- Sunroof drain blockage common — inspect if car is parked outdoors

AVERAGE ANNUAL MAINTENANCE COST
- Scheduled service: Rs. 6,000-12,000 per visit
- Annual maintenance (routine): Rs. 18,000-28,000
- With repair allowance: Rs. 22,000-35,000 per year

RESALE VALUE BENCHMARKS
- 2020 Creta SX(O) 1.5L Petrol (32,000 km): Rs. 9,00,000-10,50,000 (2024 market)
- 2020 Creta SX(O) Diesel (32,000 km): Rs. 9,50,000-11,00,000 (2024 market)
- Depreciation: 12-15% per year for first 3 years (Creta retains value well)
- Loan-free, clean history premium: Rs. 50,000-80,000 over market average

INSURANCE BENCHMARKS
- Comprehensive (2020 Creta SX(O), IDV ~9.5L): Rs. 18,000-28,000 per year
- Third-party only: Rs. 4,000-6,000 per year
