MARUTI SUZUKI SWIFT — MAINTENANCE GUIDE (2017-2024 MODELS)

OIL CHANGE INTERVAL
- Petrol variants (K12C engine): Every 10,000 km or 12 months, whichever comes first.
- Older petrol variants (K12B engine, 2011-2018): Every 5,000-7,500 km.
- CNG variants: Every 7,500 km.
- Diesel variants (DDiS 1.3L): Every 7,500 km.
For capstone estimation use 5,500 km average interval across mixed fleet.

SERVICE SCHEDULE
- 1st free service: 1,000 km or 1 month
- Paid services: Every 10,000 km (newer models) or 5,000 km (older)
- Major service (30,000 km): Replace air filter, fuel filter check, spark plugs, brake fluid check
- Major service (60,000 km): Replace timing chain tensioner check, coolant flush, spark plugs replacement
- Major service (80,000 km): Timing belt inspection (if applicable)

TYRE LIFE
- Standard: 40,000-50,000 km under normal Indian road conditions
- Replacement cycle benchmark: 40,000 km

BRAKE PAD LIFE
- Front brake pads: 30,000-40,000 km
- Rear drum brake liners: 50,000-70,000 km
- Benchmark for estimation: 30,000 km per set

COOLANT FLUSH
- Every 40,000 km or 2 years

TIMING BELT/CHAIN
- Swift uses timing chain (not belt) from 2011 onwards — no scheduled replacement
- Timing chain tensioner: Inspect at 80,000 km

KNOWN ISSUES BY YEAR
- 2011-2014 Swift: Vibration at idle due to engine mounts wearing prematurely (common at 60,000-80,000 km)
- 2015-2017 Swift: Some reports of AC compressor failure at 50,000-70,000 km
- 2018-2021 Swift (K12C): Generally reliable; fuel injector cleaning recommended at 60,000 km
- Diesel DDiS: Injector issues reported after 100,000 km; DPF filter clogging in city driving

AVERAGE ANNUAL MAINTENANCE COST (India)
- Scheduled service: Rs. 3,000-6,000 per service visit
- Annual maintenance (routine): Rs. 12,000-18,000
- With repair allowance: Rs. 15,000-22,000 per year

RESALE VALUE BENCHMARKS
- 2019 Swift VXI (45,000 km): Rs. 3,80,000-4,20,000 (2024 market)
- 2019 Swift ZXI (45,000 km): Rs. 4,20,000-4,60,000 (2024 market)
- Depreciation: Approximately 15% per year for first 3 years, then 10% per year
- Odometer-adjusted: Deduct Rs. 50-80 per extra km above expected usage

INSURANCE BENCHMARKS
- Comprehensive insurance (2019 Swift, IDV ~3.8L): Rs. 8,000-12,000 per year
- Third-party only: Rs. 2,000-3,000 per year
