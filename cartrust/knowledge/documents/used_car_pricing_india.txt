PRICE ADJUSTMENT FACTORS
=======================================================================

OWNERSHIP HISTORY
- Single owner (vs 2nd owner):            Add Rs. 20,000 – 60,000
- Third or more owner:                    Deduct Rs. 40,000 – 1,00,000
- Well-known original owner (fleet/taxi): Deduct Rs. 50,000 – 1,50,000

SERVICE HISTORY
- Full authorized service center history: Add Rs. 25,000 – 60,000
- No service records at all:              Deduct Rs. 40,000 – 80,000
- Partial/mixed ASC + local garage:       Neutral to Rs. –20,000
- Last service >12 months ago:            Deduct Rs. 15,000 – 30,000

ACCIDENT / BODY
- Minor dent / scratch (repainted panel): Deduct Rs. 20,000 – 40,000
- Undisclosed minor accident (1 panel):   Deduct Rs. 40,000 – 80,000
- Major accident (structural repair):     Deduct Rs. 1,00,000 – 2,50,000
- Flood-damaged vehicle:                  Deduct Rs. 2,00,000 – 4,00,000 (or do not buy)
- Air bag deployment repaired:            Deduct Rs. 1,00,000 – 2,00,000

ODOMETER / MILEAGE
- Every 10,000 km over expected annual:   Deduct Rs. 25,000 – 50,000
- Odometer discrepancy >10%:             Deduct Rs. 50,000 – 1,00,000
- Odometer rollback suspected:            Do not purchase

FINANCIAL / LEGAL
- Active hypothecation loan not disclosed: Do not purchase — legal risk
- Hypothecation with closure plan:         Negotiate lower by Rs. 50,000 – 1,50,000
- RC name mismatch / pending transfer:     Deduct Rs. 30,000 – 60,000 for paperwork cost
- Challan / court case pending:            Deduct Rs. 20,000 – 50,000

FUEL TYPE PREMIUMS (same year/variant)
- CNG factory-fitted over petrol:          Add Rs. 30,000 – 70,000
- Diesel over petrol (pre-2020):           Add Rs. 30,000 – 80,000
- EV over ICE (comparable segment):        Add Rs. 1,00,000 – 2,50,000

GEOGRAPHIC PREMIUM
- Mumbai / Delhi / Bengaluru premium:      5–10% over national average
- Tier-2 cities (Pune, Ahmedabad, etc.):   National average ±3%
- Tier-3 / rural markets:                  5–12% below national average

COLOUR PREMIUM
- White / Silver (best resale):            Neutral or +Rs. 5,000
- Grey / Black (popular, city):            +Rs. 5,000 – 10,000
- Red / Blue (sporty):                     –Rs. 5,000 – 10,000
- Unusual colours:                         –Rs. 10,000 – 30,000

=======================================================================
GENERAL DEPRECIATION MODEL (INDIA)
=======================================================================

STANDARD DEPRECIATION SCHEDULE
- Year 1 (0–12 months):                   15–22% of on-road price lost
- Year 2:                                  10–15% per year
- Year 3:                                  10–12% per year
- Year 4:                                  8–10% per year
- Year 5:                                  7–9% per year
- Year 6–10:                               5–8% per year
- Beyond 10 years:                         3–5% per year (floor value)

DEPRECIATION BY SEGMENT
- Entry hatchbacks (Alto, WagonR):         Slowest depreciation (8–12%/yr after yr1)
- Premium hatchbacks (Swift, i20, Baleno): 10–14%/yr
- Compact sedans (Dzire, Amaze):           11–15%/yr
- Compact SUVs (Creta, Nexon, Seltos):     8–13%/yr (high demand = slow dep.)
- Mid-size SUVs (Fortuner, Scorpio-N):     6–10%/yr (strong resale)
- MPVs (Innova Crysta):                    7–11%/yr
- Luxury (BMW, Mercedes, Audi):            18–28%/yr (fastest depreciation)

MODELS WITH HIGHEST RESALE VALUE IN INDIA (2024)
1. Maruti Suzuki (entire lineup) — consistently top resale
2. Toyota Fortuner / Innova Crysta — near-0 depreciation in years 3–6
3. Hyundai Creta (diesel AT) — best resale in compact SUV segment
4. Kia Seltos — strong first-owner resale due to brand equity
5. Mahindra Thar 4x4 — sometimes sells at or above purchase price
6. Maruti Vitara Brezza — strong due to limited supply / high demand

MILEAGE-ADJUSTED FAIR VALUE FORMULA
- Base value = current market average for model/year/variant
- For each 1,000 km above expected (12,000 km × age in years):
    Hatchbacks: Deduct Rs. 2,000–4,000
    Sedans/Compact SUVs: Deduct Rs. 3,000–5,000
    Mid-size SUVs: Deduct Rs. 4,000–7,000
    Luxury: Deduct Rs. 8,000–15,000
- For each 1,000 km below expected: Add 50–60% of the above rates
