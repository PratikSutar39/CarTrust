PRICE ADJUSTMENT FACTORS
- No service history: Deduct Rs. 30,000-60,000
- Accident history (major): Deduct Rs. 50,000-1,20,000
- Odometer discrepancy of 10,000 km: Deduct Rs. 30,000-50,000
- Active loan not disclosed: Do not purchase (legal risk)
- Single owner premium: Add Rs. 20,000-40,000
- Authorized service history: Add Rs. 20,000-40,000

GENERAL DEPRECIATION MODEL
- Year 1 loss: 15-20% of purchase price
- Subsequent years: 10-15% per year until 5 years old
- After 5 years: 7-10% per year
- Model-specific multiplier: Popular models (Swift, Creta) depreciate 2-3% slower

MILEAGE-ADJUSTED FAIR VALUE
- Base value = current market average for model/year
- For each 1,000 km over average annual usage: Deduct Rs. 3,000-5,000
- For each 1,000 km under average annual usage: Add Rs. 2,000-3,000
