CLAIM HISTORY IMPACT ON INSURANCE
- No claims bonus (NCB): 20% discount after 1 claim-free year, up to 50% after 5 years
- One major claim: NCB resets to zero; next year premium increases 15-25%
- Multiple claims: Insurer may decline renewal or apply loading

IDV CALCULATION FOR USED CAR PURCHASE
- IDV = Manufacturer's Listed Selling Price × (1 - Depreciation %)
- Depreciation: Year 1-2: 20%, Year 3: 30%, Year 4: 40%, Year 5: 50%, >5 years: 60-70%
- Actual value for Rs. 4.2L Swift (2019): IDV approximately Rs. 3.2L-3.8L in 2024

WHAT INSURANCE RECORDS REVEAL
- Claims above Rs. 50,000: Indicates major damage event
- Claim with no corresponding service record: Suggests off-books repair (structural integrity risk)
- Consecutive annual claims: Pattern of accidents or misuse
- Lapse in insurance: Legal violation; also signals ownership or financial issues

3-YEAR TOTAL COST OF OWNERSHIP MODEL
Total CoO = Purchase Price + (Annual Insurance × 3) + (Annual Maintenance × 3) + Fuel + Depreciation
For budgeting purposes, estimate:
- Annual maintenance Rs. 15,000-22,000 (Swift), Rs. 18,000-28,000 (City), Rs. 22,000-35,000 (Creta)
- Annual insurance Rs. 8,000-12,000 (Swift), Rs. 12,000-18,000 (City), Rs. 18,000-28,000 (Creta)
- Depreciation: 10-15% per year of current market value
