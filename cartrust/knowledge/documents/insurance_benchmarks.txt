INSURANCE BENCHMARKS FOR USED CARS — INDIA (2010–2026)
Last updated: May 2026
Source: IRDAI tariff, ICICI Lombard / HDFC ERGO / Bajaj Allianz / Tata AIG / Acko quotes,
PolicyBazaar / Coverfox aggregator data, May 2026.

NOTE: Comprehensive premium = own-damage + third-party + applicable add-ons.
IDV (Insured Declared Value) = Manufacturer's listed price × (1 − age depreciation).
Quoted ranges are without add-ons unless stated. Add-ons typically increase
premiums by 30–60%. Premiums vary 15–25% by city (Mumbai/Delhi highest).

=======================================================================
IRDAI DEPRECIATION SLAB FOR IDV (Used Car Insurance)
=======================================================================
- 0–6 months:          5% depreciation
- 6 months – 1 year:   15%
- 1–2 years:           20%
- 2–3 years:           30%
- 3–4 years:           40%
- 4–5 years:           50%
- 5+ years:            Mutually agreed between insurer and owner (typically 55–75%)

=======================================================================
NO CLAIM BONUS (NCB) STRUCTURE
=======================================================================
- After 1 claim-free year:               20% off OD premium
- After 2 claim-free years:              25% off
- After 3 claim-free years:              35% off
- After 4 claim-free years:              45% off
- After 5+ claim-free years:             50% off (maximum)

CLAIM IMPACT
- One small claim (<Rs. 25,000):         Consider not claiming — NCB loss > claim
- One major claim:                       NCB resets to 0; next premium up 15–25%
- Two claims in one year:                Insurer may impose 25% loading
- 3+ claims in one year:                 Risk of non-renewal or large loading

WHAT INSURANCE RECORDS REVEAL ABOUT A USED CAR
=======================================================================

CLAIM AMOUNT SIGNALS
- Claim Rs. <25,000:                     Cosmetic / minor (bumper, mirror)
- Claim Rs. 25,000 – 75,000:             Moderate (single-panel body work)
- Claim Rs. 75,000 – 1,50,000:           Major (multi-panel + paint work)
- Claim Rs. 1,50,000 – 3,00,000:         Severe (chassis / structural)
- Claim Rs. >3,00,000:                   Catastrophic (total loss / airbag deployment)

PATTERN ANALYSIS
- Claim with NO corresponding service entry → off-books repair (structural risk)
- 2+ claims within 12 months → accident-prone driver or fraud red flag
- Claim + sudden change of ownership → vehicle "flipped" after damage
- Claim during monsoon months on coastal RTO → flood damage suspicion
- Lapse in insurance >30 days → legal violation; possible ownership dispute

IDV TRENDS THAT REVEAL TAMPERING
- IDV reset to higher value mid-life → policyholder paid extra to mask claim history
- Insurance switched between 3+ insurers in 5 years → claim-shopping pattern

3-YEAR TOTAL COST OF OWNERSHIP (TCO) MODEL — BY SEGMENT
=======================================================================
TCO = Purchase Price + (3 × Annual Insurance) + (3 × Annual Maintenance)
    + Fuel cost (estimated) − Resale Value after 3 years

ANNUAL MAINTENANCE ESTIMATES (typical, well-maintained car)
- Entry hatchbacks (Alto / WagonR):      Rs. 8,000 – 14,000
- Premium hatchbacks (Swift / i20):      Rs. 15,000 – 22,000
- Compact sedans (Dzire / Amaze):        Rs. 16,000 – 24,000
- Honda City / Verna:                    Rs. 22,000 – 32,000
- Compact SUVs (Creta / Seltos / Nexon): Rs. 22,000 – 35,000
- Mid-size SUVs (XUV700 / Harrier):      Rs. 30,000 – 45,000
- Full-size SUVs (Fortuner / Endeavour): Rs. 40,000 – 65,000

- MPVs (Ertiga / Carens):                Rs. 18,000 – 28,000
- Luxury (BMW / Mercedes / Audi):        Rs. 80,000 – 2,50,000

RED-FLAG CHECKLIST — INSURANCE-RELATED ISSUES TO VERIFY BEFORE BUYING
=======================================================================
1. Pull insurance policy history for last 3–5 years.
2. Match claim dates against service log — look for unrecorded repairs.
3. Confirm current policy is active (download PDF, check expiry date).
4. Check NCB on current policy — high NCB confirms claim-free recent history.
5. Verify claim amounts — any Rs. 1L+ claim warrants a body shop inspection.
6. Look for airbag-deployment claims — structural integrity is permanently
   compromised; deduct Rs. 1.5L–3L from price or walk away.
7. Insurance switched ≥3 times in 5 years → likely claim-shopping; verify
   with all prior insurers before purchase.
8. For diesel >10 years old: comprehensive premiums rise sharply due to
   NGT diesel bans in NCR / older-vehicle restrictions in cities.
