HONDA CITY — MAINTENANCE GUIDE (2014-2023 MODELS)

OIL CHANGE INTERVAL
- Petrol variants (1.5L i-VTEC): Every 5,000-7,500 km or 6 months
- Diesel variants (1.5L i-DTEC): Every 5,000 km
- For capstone estimation use 5,500 km average interval

SERVICE SCHEDULE
- 1st free service: 1,000 km or 1 month
- Scheduled service: Every 5,000-7,500 km
- Major service (20,000 km): Spark plugs, air filter, brake fluid
- Major service (40,000 km): Timing belt inspection, coolant flush, fuel filter
- Major service (80,000 km): Timing belt replacement (CRITICAL — belt failure causes engine damage)

TIMING BELT (CRITICAL)
- Honda City (2014-2020 petrol): Uses timing belt, must be replaced at 80,000 km or 6 years
- Honda City (2020+ 5th gen): Uses timing chain, no scheduled replacement
- Failure to replace timing belt can result in catastrophic engine damage
- Replacement cost: Rs. 8,000-15,000 at authorized center

TYRE LIFE
- 40,000-50,000 km under normal conditions
- Benchmark for estimation: 40,000 km per set

BRAKE PAD LIFE
- Front disc brake pads: 25,000-35,000 km
- Benchmark for estimation: 30,000 km per set

KNOWN ISSUES BY YEAR
- 2014-2017 City: Clutch wear at 60,000-80,000 km (manual transmission)
- 2015-2018 Diesel: Timing belt must be replaced strictly at 80,000 km
- 2017-2019 City: CVT transmission fluid needs replacement at 40,000 km
- 2018-2020 City: AC refrigerant leak reported in some units at 50,000+ km

AVERAGE ANNUAL MAINTENANCE COST
- Scheduled service: Rs. 5,000-9,000 per visit
- Annual maintenance (routine): Rs. 15,000-22,000
- With repair allowance: Rs. 18,000-28,000 per year

RESALE VALUE BENCHMARKS
- 2018 City VX MT (38,000 km): Rs. 6,00,000-6,80,000 (2024 market)
- 2018 City ZX AT (38,000 km): Rs. 6,80,000-7,50,000 (2024 market)
- Depreciation: 15-18% per year for first 3 years, then 10-12%
- Premium for service history from authorized Honda dealer: Rs. 30,000-50,000 over market

INSURANCE BENCHMARKS
- Comprehensive insurance (2018 City, IDV ~6.2L): Rs. 12,000-18,000 per year
- Third-party only: Rs. 3,000-4,000 per year
