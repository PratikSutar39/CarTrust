GENERAL USED CAR MAINTENANCE NORMS — INDIA

OIL CHANGE BENCHMARKS (Indian Conditions)
- Average oil change interval across Indian passenger cars: 5,000-7,500 km
- Budget hatchbacks (Maruti, Hyundai small): 5,000-7,500 km
- Premium sedans/SUVs: 7,500-10,000 km
- Turbo engines: 5,000-7,500 km (more frequent)
- CarTrust default estimation: 5,500 km per oil change

TYRE REPLACEMENT BENCHMARKS
- Budget tyres on city roads: 35,000-45,000 km
- Premium tyres on mixed roads: 40,000-55,000 km
- CarTrust estimation benchmark: 40,000 km per set

BRAKE PAD REPLACEMENT BENCHMARKS
- City driving (heavy traffic): 25,000-35,000 km
- Mixed highway/city: 35,000-45,000 km
- CarTrust estimation benchmark: 30,000 km per set

AVERAGE ANNUAL KM DRIVEN — INDIA
- National average: 10,000-12,000 km per year
- Urban (metro cities): 12,000-15,000 km per year
- Semi-urban: 8,000-12,000 km per year
- CarTrust default: 11,000 km per year

ODOMETER ROLLBACK DETECTION
- If stated odometer < (oil_changes × 5,500): potential rollback
- If stated odometer < (vehicle_age × 8,000): suspiciously low
- Multi-signal confirmation: require 2+ independent signals to agree within 10%

TIMING BELT REPLACEMENT — GENERAL
- Vehicles with timing belt (not chain): Replace every 80,000 km or 5-6 years
- Failure to replace = risk of catastrophic engine seizure
- Cost: Rs. 6,000-20,000 depending on model

COOLANT FLUSH — GENERAL
- Every 40,000 km or 2 years (whichever first)
- Ignoring this causes overheating and cylinder head damage

TRANSMISSION SERVICE
- Manual gearbox oil: Every 40,000-60,000 km
- Automatic transmission fluid: Every 30,000-40,000 km
- CVT fluid: Every 40,000 km (often neglected, expensive to fix)

CLUTCH REPLACEMENT — MANUAL TRANSMISSION
- City driving: 60,000-80,000 km
- Highway-heavy use: 80,000-120,000 km
- Cost: Rs. 8,000-20,000

INSURANCE CLAIM THRESHOLDS
- Minor claim (cosmetic): Rs. 5,000-20,000
- Moderate claim (panel damage, airbags): Rs. 20,000-50,000
- Major claim (structural, total loss partial): Rs. 50,000+
- Total loss threshold: Usually 75% of IDV

FLOOD DAMAGE SIGNALS
- Insurance claim with no repair record
- Rust under carpets, in door hinges
- Electronic faults (cluster, window motors)
- Unusual musty smell

DEPRECIATION BENCHMARKS (INDIA)
- Year 1: 15-20% of ex-showroom
- Year 2: 10-15%
- Year 3: 10%
- Year 4-6: 8-10% per year
- Year 7+: 5-8% per year
- High-demand models (Creta, Swift): Lower depreciation by 2-3%
